Registry Model

Published units with standing record

Sovereign Standard publishes physical units in visible sequence. Each unit carries a standing record as it moves through the archive.

Deterministic origin

Every unit begins from the same fixed instrument baseline, with its sigil, hash, and generated form tied to that origin.

Recorded state

Each published change extends the standing record for that unit. What appears in the archive comes from that record.

Physical issuance

Each unit is issued as a physical object with its own artifacts, issuance record, and public control page.

Claimed state

When a collector claims a unit, that passage is added to the archive record and remains part of its visible history.
